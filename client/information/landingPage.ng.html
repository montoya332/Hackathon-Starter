Weekly Reports

View attendance summaries and keep track of your program’s record with ease from anywhere on any device. Give and record surveys which assess program performance and student growth throughout a desired time period.

Student Records

Keep your team connected! Student’s records, such as grades and contact information, are kept on an easy to use network which allows you to update and view these records from multiple devices at the same time.

About

Founder Arturo Montoya is a proud to be the first in his family to graduate from college with a B.S. in Computer Engineering from San Jose State University. Arturo originally comes from a background of working with underserved youth in the south bay and the peninsula. From his time at San Jose State University emerged with a passion for driving student success through efficiency and innovation. Over the last year he has worked on various projects aiming to improve tracking efficiency for both the non-profit and government sectors.
